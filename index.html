Charlie Wu

I am a designer, developer, photographer, and a student. I have a passion for simplifying the complex.

I am currently completing my undergraduate studies in Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, Canada. Systems Design taught me to take multi-disciplinary approaches to design and problem solving, so that I am able to develop solutions that are both intuitive and comfortable for the end-user. Life is always moving, and so should art and design.
