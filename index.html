About Me

"Ideas are cheap, execution is everything."

Who am I?

Raised in Toronto and Waterloo, Ontario, I started my journey learning about systems (human, mechanical, and digital). I discovered how things worked in different industries, but what caught my attention was how inefficient they sometimes were. From that point on, I sought to eliminate confusing interfaces and create better products.

I love understanding the nitty-gritty details of various problems, and I also love creating solutions for said problems. I do so by conducting research, analyzing tasks, creating wireframes & prototypes, and developing code.
