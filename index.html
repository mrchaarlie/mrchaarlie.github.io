About

I am a student -- not just in school, but also in aspects that pertain to life, technology, and character. I do not let my limitations define me, nor by some marks on a paper. I hope my work and actions will let me impact the world.

I am currently completing my undergraduate studies in Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, Canada. Systems Design taught me to take multi-disciplinary approaches to design and problem solving, so that I am able to develop solutions that are both intuitive and comfortable for the end-user. I am a user experience designer and developer that stays up to date with modern trends - life is always moving, and so should art and design.

You can see some of my work on my Behance portfolio, or contact me via e-mail.
