Job Tracker Purpose

Users were having trouble making efficient use of the job tracker in a table format, which was caused by tedious navigation and dense display.

Job Tracker's Board View.

I facilitated a week-long design sprint with the dev and product team to search for better solutions. After much ideation and deliberation over 2 days, we ended up with a rough storyboard outline for the new feature.

A storyboard created during the design sprint.

The team spent a day collaboratively putting ideas and designs together in Figma, under my guidance. I refined and brought the pieces together to form a cohesive interactive prototype. I then carefully designed the script and questionnaire, and conducted some guerilla user testing.
